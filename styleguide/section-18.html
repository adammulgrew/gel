Responsive video

You may need to embed video from other third party websites into your website. To keep the video responsive it needs to sit in it's own <div class="flex-video">. Just replace the link with your own.

<div class="flex-video flex-video--widescreen">
<iframe width="1280" height="720" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/0TwCFfW_Has" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe>
</div>
